GENERAL RULES
  • If the scheduled venue is changed, all bets will be considered void.
  • If a game is abandoned during the 1st half, all 1st half bets are considered void. If a game is abandoned during the 2nd half, all 2nd half bets are considered void,all 1st half bets will still be valid.unless otherwise stated in the individual bet type rules.
  • For Quarter / Half Betting, the period must be completed for bets to be valid, unless explicitly stated below or in the individual Bet Type rules.
  • NCAA Venue Rule: Please note, that the “Home” and “Away” venue indicated on the website is for reference only.Bets will stand regardless of change of venue, be it to the 'Home' team's venue, the 'Away' team's venue or to a 'Neutral' venue.
  • If the game starts before the scheduled time, only bets placed before the game commenced will be considered valid. Bets placed after the game commenced will be considered void. This excludes In-Play bet types.
  • Overtime is included for any Game, 2nd Half betting or 2-leg tied match, unless otherwise stated. 4th Quarter betting does not include overtime
  • If a match does not start on the scheduled start date then all bets will be void unless the match is started or re-started within twelve hours of the original start time (unless stated otherwise).
  • If the match is suspended or abandoned off within 12 hours of the start of the specified time, but at least 90% of the game time has been completed (e.g. 36 minutes of a 40-minute game; or 43 minutes of a 48-minute game) , full-time bets still stand.Otherwise the bet slip on the suspended or suspended event will be void, except for the unconditional decision of the bet result.
  • All basketball matches will be settled on the official declared result including any overtime.
  • For 3 x 3 Basketball, settlement will be based on official competition rules.
BET TYPES

Winner

  • Predict who will win the game. This market will contain the two teams.
  • The match market includes the full time game.

1X2

  • Predict which team will win or whether the result between both teams will be a draw.This market will contain the two teams and a draw as betting options.
  • Markets offered include regular time, match halves or quarter Betting.

Handicap

  • Predict which team will win the match halves /full time/quarter with the indicated handicap applied.
  • If the game is suspended or cancelled during the 2nd half, all 1st half bets will still be valid.
  • If the game is abandoned during the 2nd half, all 2nd half bets will be void unless settlement has been confirmed.
  • Markets offered include Full Game, Match Halves or Quarter Betting.

Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored will be over or under the indicated total line.
  • Markets offered include Full Game, Match Halves or Quarter Betting.
  • If a game is abandoned, Over / Under bets will only be settled when the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points have no affect on the market result. In all other scenarios, bets will be considered void.
  • If the game is abandoned during the 1st half, all 1st half bets will be void unless they have been unconditionally determined as any further potential points have no affect on the market result.
  • If the game is abandoned during the 2nd half, all 1st half bets will still be valid.
  • If the game is abandoned during the 2nd half, all 2nd half bets will be void unless settlement has been confirmed.
  • If the game is abandoned, all period bets will be void unless one occurs:
    • a.The period was already completed before the game was abandoned.
    • b.Before the game is abandoned the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points have no affect on the market result.
  • In Play Settlement is based on the final score line and the total line is applied to a 0-0 score line

Totals Exact

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored will be over or under or equal the market specified indicated total line.
  • Markets offered include Full Game, Match Halves or Quarter Betting.
  • If a game is abandoned, Over / Under bets will only be settled when the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points have no affect on the market result. In all other scenarios, bets will be considered void.
  • If the game is abandoned during the 1st half, all 1st half bets will be void unless they have been unconditionally determined as any further potential points have no affect on the market result.
  • If the game is abandoned during the 2nd half, all 1st half bets will still be valid.
  • If the game is abandoned during the 2nd half, all 2nd half bets will be void unless they have been unconditionally determined as any further potential points have no affect on the market result.
  • If the game is abandoned, all period bets will be void unless one occurs:
    • The period was already completed before the game was abandoned.
    • Before the game is abandoned the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points have no affect on the market result.

Highest Scoring Quarter

  • Predict which quarter will score the most points.
  • Overtime does not count.
  • If the match is suspended or abandoned, all bets on the highest scoring quarter will be considered void.
  • If two or more quarters have the same highest score, a “draw” will be settled as a win.

Quarter Winner Margin

  • Winner and score difference after a single quarter End.
  • Betting on the home team with a margin of 3+ is predicted to win the game, and the difference is equal to or greater than 3.
  • Betting on the away team with a margin of 3+ is predicted to win the game, and the difference is equal to or greater than 3.
  • Bet on other predicted difference between the home and away teams is less than 3.

Winning Margin 26+

  • Winner of the whole game (including overtime) and the score difference between the two teams.
  • Predict the home team or the away team to win by 1-5 points (inclusive of 1 to 5 points), 6-10 points (inclusive of 6 to 10 points), 11-15 points (inclusive of 11 to 15 points), 16-20 Points (including 16 points to 20 points), 21-25 points (including 21 points to 25 points).
  • Predict the home team or the away team to win 26+, and the difference is equal to or greater than 26.
